# Sales-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard-Using-Power-BI
This repository contains a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail sales and profitability across various dimensions including geography, time, and product categories. The goal was to provide actionable insights for stakeholders to optimize regional strategies and inventory management.

#Insights
Executive Summary: High-level KPIs for total revenue ($2.3M) and net profit ($292K). 
Regional Distribution: A Donut Chart breakdown showing that the West and East regions account for over 60% of total sales. 
Monthly Performance: A combo chart (Line & Clustered Column) analyzing the relationship between the Sum of Profit and Sum of Sales from January through December. 
Product Deep-Dive: A horizontal bar chart identifying the Top 10 products by sales volume, led by the Canon imageCLASS series. 
Geographical Visuals: An interactive map of North America showcasing sales distribution by state/province. 


#Tools Used:
Power BI Desktop: For report authoring and dashboard design.
Power Query: For data cleaning and transformation (ETL).
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): For creating calculated measures and KPIs.
